1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the STAR Emergency App ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name: First and last name
  • Email address
  • Emergency contact number
  • Account number or alias
  • Device location (GPS) – collected temporarily during SOS activation

3. How We Use Your Information

Location data is used only at the moment of SOS activation to inform nearby emergency services. Personal data is stored securely and used only to help coordinate emergency responses.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps API – to capture GPS coordinates
  • Google Sign-In – to authenticate users

These services are governed by their own policies: Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention and Security

GPS location is not stored after the SOS alert is completed. Personal data is stored securely with encryption. We use HTTPS and secure WebSocket protocols to protect user data.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
